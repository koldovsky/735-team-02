FRUITY

AMERICAN PALE ALE

American pale ale is similar to Indian pale ale, but in this case, only American-made hops and malt are used. At the same time, APA is characterized by a stunning combination of citrus notes: grapefruit, lemon, orange, as well as hop bitterness, traditional for ale.

Meet Our Beer and Make Your Choice.
If you have any questions or suggestions, you can always contact us directly.

Can I order your beer online?

You can place an order by emailing us. Our manager will contact you and clarify all the details.

Do you sell only bottled or draft beer too?

You can buy bottled, canned, and draft beer.

Do you have Blanche beer?

Of course. We have over 20 different types of beer, you can order any of them.

Do I need to sign up in advance for a tour or tasting?

Yes, we recommend that you book tours and tastings at least a few weeks in advance.
